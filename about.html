My Bio

Hello, my name is Hugo.

I live on Terry Lou Ave and I am a great bike fanatic.

Since I have been a kid I loved bikes.

My love for bikes grew in Canada where I found my freedom in the nearest parking lot to our house.

After Canada, me and my family moved to Miami.

As some of you may know Miami and Bikes don't go very well together.

In 2023, 969 total bicycle crashes have been recorded solely in Miami-Dade County.

Miami in fact, has one of the highest rates of bicycle-related injuries and fatalities in the United States.

That's crazy to think about. So of course, I as a 10 year old could not resort to bikes for freedom.

The more I grew the more I grew impatient to hop on a bicycle.

As soon as my parents told me we were moving to Mass, my heart leaped out of my chest.

To conclude, ever since I have been here I have done a lot of bike riding and repairing.

I recently joined a group called the Falmouth Bike Lab.

I volunteer there every Thursday to help with repairs and giveaways, so if you want to swing by, you can.

Every now and then you will see me riding a different bike every time on Terry Lou Ave.

Whether it's a huge Mountain Bike (MTB) or a small pink little girl's bike.

Looking forward to seeing you at:
